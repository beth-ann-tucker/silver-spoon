Beta Alpha Psi Score Card

As president of Beta Alpha Psi, an accounting honor society, I frequenly go to bap.org to view requirement for our club. BAP chapters can earn three different status levels, non-award seeking, distinguished, and superior. The way that BAP has the score card set up it shows requirements for all three levels no matter what. Not only is this confusing and more difficult to read, but it also requires mental math (and even though we are accountants, we try to avoid doing math).

Beth Ann's Version of the Beta Alpha Psi Score Card

If BAP would allow me to redesign the score card, I would have it were the user can toggle between the different levels. This makes it where no unneccessary information is displayed and most importantly, no mental math required. Additionally, this formats highlights the total making it easier to find.
